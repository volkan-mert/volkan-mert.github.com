About Me:

Volkan Mert was born in Nilüfer, Bursa, Türkiye in 1984. He received B.S. degrees in industrial engineering in 2006 and in electrical and electronics engineering in 2007, both from Anadolu University, Eskişehir. He subsequently obtained a M.S. degree in electrical and electronics engineering from TOBB Economy and Technology University, Ankara, in 2022. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in aeronautical and astronautical engineering at Istanbul Technical University. He has been with Turkish Aerospace since 2010. From 2010 to 2022, he held various engineering roles in prominent helicopter projects, including positions in manufacturing, electrical systems design, and autopilot systems design. Since 2022, he has been employed as a control and simulation engineer, and subsequently as a research engineer, at Istanbul Technical University within Turkish Aerospace, where he is currently pursuing his Ph.D. His research interests include Model-Based Observers (MBO), Hybrid-kalman Filters (HKF), active sidestick control systems and battery management systems.
